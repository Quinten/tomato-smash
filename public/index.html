Create a bookmark with any funny name and paste this into the url field.

javascript:(_=>{import('https://quinten.github.io/tomato-smash/index.js').then(_=>{new TomatoSmash.Game()})})()

Then click the bookmark while you are on any site.

Tomatos will be thrown up and you can smash them to ketchup with your mouse or tap finger.
